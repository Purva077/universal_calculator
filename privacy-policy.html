Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Introduction

Universal Calculator Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our calculator application.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Our app operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track your usage
  • Store your calculations on our servers
  • Require account creation or login
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Share data with third parties

Local Storage

The app uses your device's local storage to save:

  • Your favorite calculators
  • Calculation history (stored only on your device)
  • App preferences (theme, settings)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data or cache.

Internet Connectivity

The app requires internet connection only for:

  • Live Currency Converter: Fetches real-time exchange rates from public APIs (frankfurter.app and open.er-api.com)
  • Initial Download: To install the app and its resources

After installation, the app works completely offline. Currency rates are cached for 10 minutes to minimize API calls.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party APIs:

  • Frankfurter API (https://www.frankfurter.app) - For currency exchange rates
  • ExchangeRate API (https://open.er-api.com) - Fallback for currency rates

These services may have their own privacy policies. We do not send any personal information to these services.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your data on our servers, there is no risk of data breaches from our end. All your data remains on your device under your control.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear history and favorites anytime within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Use the app offline to avoid any network requests

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Open Source

Universal Calculator Hub is open source. You can review our code to verify our privacy practices.

Email: support@calchub.app

Summary

Your privacy is 100% protected because we don't collect any data.

  • ✅ No tracking or analytics
